Biography

Allan Freddy was born on the 20th of June 2002 to parents E M and F Freddy. He grew up in Mash East province where he spent much of his childhood and attended high school at KHS. Currently he resides in Mash Central and attends a web development course offered by Learnio Academy at SOS Children's home.


Education

Allan Freddy attended his primary, secondary and advanced level education at Kambarami High School. An introverted and socially inept child, he excelled in almost all subjects with his supremacy in Mathematics and Science subjects.

He attended a brief course in Computer Operations and Packages offered by HEXCO and got distinctively certified while still doing his Ordinary level studies. He studied four practical subjects which included Textile Technolgy ad Design which has geatly influenced his passion for fashion. Allan freddy completed his ordinary level studies in 2018 and began his advanced level studies in 2019 studying science subjects.Despite interruptions by the COVID 19 pandemic, he managed to complete this course in early 2021.

Inspired by influential businessmen like Robert Kiyosaki, he refused to attend college and chose to take on a business career instead.


Career

In late 2021, Allan Freddy began working for a clothing store in Manhenga, Bindura, as a salesman and later left the store in mid 2022 to go on and open his own store in Goromonzi, Zimbabwe. He operated in Goromonzi for almost a year and later moved back to Manhenga to set up the store there, where it is currently running.

He started attending a web development course offered by Learnio Academy online at SOS Children's Home village in Chiwaridzo, Bindura, in late September 2024. Currently he does front-end web development in HTML, CSS and Javascript at the mentioned place.
